Privacy Policy - Bing Search Bot

Last updated: November 15, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Bing Search Bot ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you log in with your Microsoft account, we access your Microsoft account only to allow you to earn rewards points. We do not store your Microsoft credentials.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the application, including search queries performed and rewards points earned.
  • Device Information: We collect device-specific information such as your device model, operating system version, and browser type to optimize the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate automated searches through Bing to help earn Microsoft Rewards points
  • Track your search progress and rewards points
  • Improve and optimize the application
  • Provide technical support

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy:

  • With Microsoft services when you perform searches (subject to Microsoft's own privacy policy)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With service providers who help us operate the application (such as hosting and analytics providers)

Advertising

Our app contains advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and personal data for personalized advertising. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information about how they handle your data.

Data Retention

We store your usage data locally on your device and only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You can clear this data by clearing the app's data through your device settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing your data
  • Correcting inaccurate data
  • Deleting your data
  • Restricting or objecting to processing

You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
